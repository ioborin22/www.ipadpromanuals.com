Screen Mirroring iPhone or iPad app on TV with stereo sound

This just in. Thanks to iOS 13 now all AirBeamTV mirror apps for iPhone and iPad supports stereo sound in Safari browser! With more on this story, it's me. Oh yes it's true, iOS 13 has answered our prayers and we can now have stereo sound in Safari browser so all we need to do is check that we have iOS 13. So, grab your iPhone or iPad, open settings, scroll down to general and click about and it will say software version on mine 13 point one point two. If you click 'Software Update', you can find out if you can update to iOS 13 i you're on a lower version. So, I'm on iOS 13, all I need to do now is open the mirror app. I'm using an LG TV with a Chromecast Ultra. This app has also been upgraded so the latency is even less now, great news. So all I need to do on my iPhone is open the Mirror for Chromecast app, find my Chromecast, hit 'Mirror Chromecast' and wait the short time until my screen appears over here...

Sure enough, there he is. So let's open Safari..finally and here I have a video on Vimeo which I can hit play on. All I need to do is make sure the sound is turned down and the mute switch is disabled so it's not muted. I can turn the sound up on here and as you can hear sweet sweet stereo sound as ever by putting it into full screen on my phone turning it on its side the same thing will happen on this screen. You can see an Instagram notification there, I'm very popular. And thanks to the stereo sound now perhaps you might want to play music. Say your TV's hooked up to a nice sound system a sound bar. We can open up Spotify in this instance play some music through here... Stereo sound through our TV...Nice!
