Protecting Your iPad Pro

Exactly, once you buy iPad Pro, you need to protect it to make it last longer since it is precious. Here are the ways to care your iPad Pro.

  1. Buy a screen protector and a case. The first thing you have to do when you have bought new iPad Pro is purchasing the iPad case and its screen protector. These two things are beneficial to avoid any damage or physical harm when you carelessly drop your iPad.
  2. Use the Passcode Lock Function. As you buy iPad, you must not want anyone else, but you use it. Therefore, you need to do something to keep your privacy. Setting the Passcode lock function will be a good idea. You just need to go to the setting icon, turn the Passcode on and set it as you want.
  3. Back up the iPad Pro regularly. Sometimes, you must be careless of deleting your important data or file. Then, you might be frustrated and do not know what to do to get your file back. If you back up your phone often, you could overcome problems such as failed iOS upgrade, mistaken deletion, troubled data transferring, etc.
