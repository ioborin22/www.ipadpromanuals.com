The iPad Pro is a state-of-the-art tablet computer engineered, created, and marketed by Apple Inc, a globally recognized American technology giant.

Introduced with great fanfare on September 9, 2015, and officially released on November 11, 2015, the iPad Pro marked a significant milestone in tablet technology. It boasts a stunning 12.9-inch display, making it larger than any of its predecessors. Customers have the option to choose from three configurations: Wi-Fi-only models with either 32 GB or 128 GB of storage space, or a 128 GB model equipped with both Wi-Fi and cellular connectivity. Furthermore, the iPad Pro has the distinction of being the first iPad to incorporate LPDDR4 RAM.
